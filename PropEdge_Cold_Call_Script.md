# PropEdge AI: Conversational Cold Call Script
*(Natural, Friendly, Problem-First Approach)*

## 1. The Friendly Intro
**Goal:** Sound relaxed, confident, and like a normal person (not a salesperson).

**You:** "Hey [Agent Name], this is [Your Name]. I’m actually a software developer, not a salesperson. I know you weren't expecting my call today—do you have a quick 60 seconds?"

*(Wait for them to say "Sure" or "What's this about?")*

---

## 2. Highlighting the Reality (The Problem & Curiosity)
**Goal:** Make them realize they are losing money right now. Ask a question that makes them think about their own business.

**You:** "Thanks. So, the reason I'm reaching out is I’ve been looking closely at how local real estate teams handle incoming leads. I'm curious—when a new lead comes in from your website or Facebook, especially late at night or when your agents are busy on other calls... what usually happens? How long does it take to call them back?"

*(Let them answer. They will usually admit it takes a few hours or until the next morning.)*

**You:** "Right, that's what I keep hearing. And the reality is, if a lead isn't called back within 5 minutes, they are already looking at another agent's property. It's frustrating because you're spending money to get those leads, but they are slipping through the cracks just because of timing."

---

## 3. The Simple Solution
**Goal:** Explain how your system steps in to fix that exact pain point, using simple, everyday words.

**You:** "Since I'm a developer, I decided to build something to fix that exact problem. 

I built a system that connects to your leads. The second a lead submits their info, my system calls them back in under 10 seconds. It actually talks to them in a completely natural, human voice. It asks what their budget is, what kind of property they want, and if they are ready, it books a site visit directly into your calendar.

If they are busy and don't pick up, it instantly sends them an email with property details so they don't go cold."

---

## 4. The Low-Pressure Demo Ask
**Goal:** Book the meeting by showing respect for their time.

**You:** "Basically, it handles all the chasing and following up, so your agents can just focus on meeting people and closing deals. 

I’d love to just show you my screen for 10 minutes and let you hear how real the voice sounds. It's much easier to understand when you see it. Would you be open to a quick 10-minute demo this Thursday or Friday?"

---

## 5. Handling Questions
*   **"Is this an automated robot?"** 
    "It is AI, but it doesn't sound like those old, robotic phone trees. It pauses, it listens, and it knows your actual property listings. That's why I'd love to just let you hear it for 10 minutes."
*   **"We already have a CRM/Team."** 
    "That's great, this actually works *with* your team. It just does the immediate 10-second follow-up so your agents aren't stressed trying to call people back instantly while they are out on site visits."
